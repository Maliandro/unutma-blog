## The three-list method

**Weekly planning tips** that work in real life: (1) calendar truth — what is immovable; (2) top three outcomes for the week; (3) a “later” list so shiny tasks do not hijack Monday.

## Protect deep work

Block two 90-minute windows before the week fills with meetings. Say no with a redirect: “I can do X on Thursday.”
