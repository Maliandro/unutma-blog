## Local-first is a feature

**Privacy-first productivity apps** should explain clearly what leaves your device. If the answer is “everything syncs by default,” ask whether you need that trade-off for notes and tasks.

## Checklist

- **Export** in open formats.
- **Encryption** at rest on mobile devices.
- **Optional** account for premium features, not for basic notes.
- **Permissions** that match the job (a journal app rarely needs your contacts).
