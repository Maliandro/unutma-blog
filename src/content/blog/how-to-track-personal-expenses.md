## Start with categories you will actually use

**How to track personal expenses** begins with 5–7 categories — not thirty. Food, housing, transport, subscriptions, savings, fun, and miscellaneous cover most lives.

## Weekly, not daily, for sanity

Check totals weekly. Daily micromanagement helps some people — and burns out others. Pick the rhythm you will still use in a busy month.

## Privacy note

If you prefer **not** syncing bank data to the cloud, manual entry in an offline-friendly app keeps control on-device. Pair this with ideas from [digital minimalism tips](/en/blog/digital-minimalism-tips/).

## Key takeaways

Expense tracking is for awareness first, optimization second.
