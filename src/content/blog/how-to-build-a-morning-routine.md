## Anchor habits first

**How to build a morning routine** starts with anchors you already do: coffee, shower, walking the dog. Stack new habits after anchors, not before alarms you already snooze.

## One meaningful task

Before email, move one project forward for 25 minutes. Protect it like a meeting with yourself.
